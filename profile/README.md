## 🏢 About Us

**Morissua** is a software development company focused on delivering high-quality, scalable, and modern digital solutions. We partner with businesses to design, build, and maintain software that drives real impact.

We believe great software is built on three pillars: **clarity of purpose**, **engineering excellence**, and **long-term thinking**.

## 🛠️ Our Tech Stack

We are **technology-agnostic** — we choose the right tool for each project rather than committing to a fixed stack. Our team is experienced across a wide range of technologies and adapts to the needs of every client.

| Layer | Approach |
|-------|----------|
| 🖥️ **Frontend** | Modern web technologies and frameworks, chosen per project requirements |
| 📱 **Mobile** | Native and cross-platform solutions for iOS & Android |
| ⚙️ **Backend** | Scalable server-side architectures using the most suitable languages and frameworks |
| 🗄️ **Database** | Relational and non-relational databases based on data requirements |
| ☁️ **Cloud & DevOps** | Multi-cloud deployments, containerization, CI/CD automation |

## 🤝 Working With Us

Interested in partnering with Morissua? Here's how we typically engage:

1. **Discovery Call** — We learn about your goals, constraints, and timeline
